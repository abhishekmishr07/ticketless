About UPSRTC

Committed to providing safe, reliable, and affordable transport for Uttar Pradesh

Who We Are

Uttar Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation (UPSRTC) is the state-owned road transport service of Uttar Pradesh. Established to provide affordable, safe, and convenient public transportation, UPSRTC serves millions of passengers daily across the state and neighboring regions.

With innovations like the Ticketless Bus System, UPSRTC is stepping into the future of digital travel. Our mission is to make journeys seamless, eco-friendly, and passenger-centric while ensuring world-class service standards.
